§ 001 — The World

Survival kingdom.
Engineer's brain.

Outerworlds is a Minecraft survival SMP for people who take building seriously and still think giant rotating machinery is extremely cool. It is survival at heart, but tuned for players who want clean aesthetics, ambitious infrastructure, and a world that feels crafted instead of spammed into existence.

So yes, we absolutely understand the Minecraft species that automates everything. If your first instinct is to over-engineer a farm, optimize a workshop, or move an unhealthy amount of stone with belts and gearboxes, you are among friends.

§ 003 — Why This Server

Built for planners,
builders, and lunatics with spreadsheets.

Creative Workflow

Design in creative. Build in survival.

We have a creative version of the server specifically so you can prototype districts, test palettes, draft megabases, and solve the awkward part where roofs look terrible until the seventh attempt.

Once the plan is right, use Litematica to turn it into a schematic and bring it into the survival world like a civilized wizard-engineer.

Builder Culture

Pretty factories. Serious standards.

This is a builder-first community. We love technical players, but not ugly machine spam. The goal is not just automation. The goal is automation that looks like it deserves a cinematic flyover.

Come for the farms, stay because someone convinced you the retaining walls need three more block palettes and, annoyingly, they were right.

Render Distance Sorcery

Voxy makes the world feel enormous.

We also support Voxy, a lightweight LoD mod that can render absurdly long distances on supported systems. Explore once, build forever, and actually admire the skyline you spent thirty hours perfecting.

It is the kind of feature that turns a base into a kingdom and a mountain range into a proper backdrop.

§ 006 — Commerce
